Open-source intelligence (OSINT) and in-depth case studies on West African scam networks, threat actor TTPs, and the underground cybercrime economy.

## About This Repository

This repository contains detailed analyses, Indicators of Compromise (IOCs), and detection methodologies focused on understanding and disrupting organized cybercrime operations originating from West Africa. Our research covers:

- **Social Engineering Ecosystems**
- **Threat Actor TTPs** (Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures)
- **Cryptocurrency Tracking & Analysis**
- **Infrastructure Mapping**
- **Network Analysis & Attribution**
- **Countermeasures & Mitigation Strategies**

## Objectives

- Provide actionable intelligence for security professionals
- Enhance public awareness through detailed case studies
- Develop pattern recognition models for scam detection
- Support law enforcement and platform providers with technical analysis
- Foster collaboration in the fight against organized cybercrime
